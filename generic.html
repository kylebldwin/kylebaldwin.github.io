Ideal Job - IT Lecturer

A position at RMIT University to teach and educate students studying Business Information Systems in either an undergraduate or masters course.
The position appeals to me as I would love to teach what I am passionate about in the future and I have gotten some inspiration from my father who is a lecturer for Computer Science at The University of Melbourne.
The position requires a lot of qualifications which will take a very long time to get such as a PhD in the given subject as well as prior teaching at an Australian university within a given timeframe of 3 years.
I am definitely unqualified for this job at the moment, as I am certain that I wouldn’t be able to teach the subject and in addition, not only do I not have a PhD, I haven’t even finished the first year of my bachelors degree.
I will obtain these qualifications by studying hard and completing my degree and eventually work my way up to a PhD which may take me many years.
